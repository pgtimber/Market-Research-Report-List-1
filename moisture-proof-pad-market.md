<p><p>Moisture-proof pads are primarily available in five main types in the market - Inflatable Type, Open Chamber Type, Closed Chamber Type, Self-inflating Type, and Others. Inflatable pads require manual inflation using a pump or breath, Open Chamber pads have individual air compartments for better weight distribution, Closed Chamber pads have interconnected air chambers to prevent air from shifting, Self-inflating pads use open-cell foam that expands when the valve is opened, and Other types include unique designs or materials for specific purposes. Each type offers different features and benefits for users seeking moisture-proof padding during outdoor activities.</p></p>

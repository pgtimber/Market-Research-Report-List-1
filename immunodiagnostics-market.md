Immunodiagnostics plays a crucial role in the early detection, monitoring, and management of diseases.</p><p>The global immunodiagnostics market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.00% during the forecast period. <p><p>Immunodiagnostics is a field within diagnostic medicine that focuses on detecting and measuring specific molecules within our immune system. This market can be categorized into two types: immunodiagnostic instruments and immunoassays. Immunodiagnostic instruments refer to the devices and equipment used to analyze and interpret immune system responses, such as automated analyzers. On the other hand, immunoassays are tests conducted to measure the concentration of specific molecules, like antibodies or antigens, in a biological sample, providing valuable information for diagnosing diseases and monitoring patients' immune responses.</p></p>

<p><p>The global external temporary pacemaker market includes two main types: Dual Chamber External Temporary Pacemaker and Single Chamber External Temporary Pacemaker. Dual chamber pacemakers are designed to monitor and regulate the rhythms of both the atria and ventricles of the heart, offering more precise control over the heart's electrical activity. Single chamber pacemakers, on the other hand, only monitor and regulate either the atria or ventricles. Both types of external temporary pacemakers are crucial in managing conditions such as bradycardia and heart block.</p></p>

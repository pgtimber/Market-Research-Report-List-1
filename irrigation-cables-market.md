<p><strong>The Irrigation Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Core</li><li>Multi-core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Irrigation cables are essential components for transmitting power and control signals in automated irrigation systems. Single core cables have only one conducting core, making them ideal for simple applications with lower power requirements. On the other hand, multi-core cables feature multiple conducting cores bundled together, allowing for more complex systems with higher power needs. While single core cables are more common in smaller scale irrigation setups, multi-core cables are preferred for larger and more sophisticated irrigation systems.</p></p>

<p><p>Loose fill polystyrene packaging is available in various types such as ordinary polystyrene, expanded polystyrene (EPS), high impact polystyrene (HIPS), and specialty polystyrene (SPS). Ordinary polystyrene is a basic form of packaging material, while EPS is known for its lightweight and insulating properties. HIPS offers high impact resistance, making it suitable for fragile items. SPS is a specialty type of polystyrene designed for specific applications, such as food packaging or medical supplies. Each type caters to different packaging needs in the market.</p></p>

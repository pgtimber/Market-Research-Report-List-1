<p><p>Charge Control Agents (CCA) are used in various industries to control the charge of pigment particles in coatings and inks. These agents come in two types: Negatively Charged N Series and Positively Charged P Series. N Series CCAs are commonly used in water-based systems where pigment particles have a negative charge, while P Series CCAs are used in solvent-based systems where pigment particles have a positive charge. Each type of CCA is designed to effectively control the charge of pigment particles in different types of formulations.</p></p>

Additionally, the growing demand for minimally invasive surgeries and the increasing preference for endoscopic stenting procedures over traditional surgical interventions also contribute to the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the emergence of bio-absorbable stents, which eliminates the need for a second procedure to remove the stent, is driving market growth.

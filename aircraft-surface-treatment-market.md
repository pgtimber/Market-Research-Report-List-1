<p><p>Aircraft surface treatment market involves various types such as pre-treatment, chemical milling, depaint and repaint, and engine maintenance. Pre-treatment involves cleaning and preparing the surface for further treatment. Chemical milling is a process of selectively removing material from a part using chemicals. Depaint and repaint is the process of removing old paint, preparing the surface, and applying new paint. Engine maintenance involves cleaning and maintaining the engines of aircraft to ensure optimal performance and longevity. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining and enhancing the quality of aircraft surfaces.</p></p>

<p><strong>The RFID Tag Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HF RFID Chip</li><li>UHF RFID Chip</li><li>LF RFID Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID tag chips are categorized into three types - HF RFID chips, UHF RFID chips, and LF RFID chips. HF RFID chips operate at high frequencies and are commonly used in applications such as access control and payment systems. UHF RFID chips operate at ultra-high frequencies and are ideal for inventory management and supply chain applications due to their long read range. LF RFID chips operate at low frequencies and are suitable for applications requiring close proximity reading, such as animal tracking and keyless entry systems.</p></p>

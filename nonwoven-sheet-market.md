<p><ul><li>Meltblown Type</li><li>Spunbonded Type</li><li>Spunlace Type</li><li>Needle Punch Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nonwoven sheet market consists of various types such as Meltblown Type, Spunbonded Type, Spunlace Type, Needle Punch Type, and Others. Meltblown type sheets are made using high-velocity hot air to form fine fibers. Spunbonded type sheets are created by spinning continuous filaments. Spunlace type sheets are produced by entangling fibers through high-pressure water jets. Needle Punch type sheets are created by mechanically interlocking fibers. The "Others" category includes unique nonwoven sheet types that do not fall into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>

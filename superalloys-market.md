<p><ul><li>Nickel Based</li><li>Nickel-Iron Based</li><li>Cobalt-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Superalloys refer to a group of high-performance materials designed to withstand extreme temperatures, corrosion, and stress. There are three main types of superalloys: Nickel-based, Nickel-Iron based, and Cobalt-based. Nickel-based superalloys are known for their high strength and resistance to heat, making them ideal for aerospace and gas turbine applications. Nickel-Iron based superalloys offer good resistance to oxidation and corrosion at high temperatures. Cobalt-based superalloys are preferred for their excellent high-temperature strength and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for jet engine components and industrial gas turbines.</p></p>

<p><p>Refractory clay is categorized into two main types: hard clay and soft clay. Hard clay, also known as fireclay, is a dense and highly durable material that can withstand high temperatures, making it ideal for applications in industries such as metalworking and ceramics. Soft clay, on the other hand, is more malleable and easier to work with, making it suitable for shaping into intricate designs. Both types of refractory clay play crucial roles in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Polyurethane Dispersions (PUD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Component Polyurethane</li><li>Two-Component Polyurethane</li><li>Urethane-modified</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane Dispersions (PUD) are widely used in various industries due to their versatility and durability. One-Component Polyurethane is a self-crosslinking system that requires no additional components, making it convenient for applications such as coatings and adhesives. Two-Component Polyurethane involves mixing two components to initiate the curing process, providing a high level of customization and flexibility for different applications. Urethane-modified PUD combines the advantages of polyurethane and urethane, offering enhanced performance and properties for applications requiring more specific requirements.</p></p>

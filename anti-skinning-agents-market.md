<p><p>Anti-skinning agents are chemicals used in coatings and paints to prevent the formation of skin on their surface when exposed to air. The market for anti-skinning agents can be divided into two main types: oxime and phenols. Oxime agents are known for their ability to react with oxygen to prevent skin formation, while phenols are effective antioxidants that inhibit the oxidation process. Both types of agents play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and shelf life of coatings and paints by preventing skinning and maintaining their usability.</p></p>

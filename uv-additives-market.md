<p><p>UV additives are chemicals added to materials to protect them from the damaging effects of UV radiation. Three main types of UV additives are commonly used in the market: heat stabilizers, light stabilizers, and UV absorbers. Heat stabilizers help prevent materials from degrading under high temperatures, while light stabilizers protect against degradation from visible and UV light. UV absorbers absorb UV radiation and convert it into harmless energy, reducing the potential for damage to the material.</p></p>

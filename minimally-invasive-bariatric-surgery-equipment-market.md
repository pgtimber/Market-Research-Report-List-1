<p><ul><li>Assisting Devices</li><li>Implantable Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The minimally invasive bariatric surgery equipment market includes two main types: assisting devices and implantable devices. Assisting devices are used during the surgical procedure to enhance the surgeon's precision and effectiveness, such as laparoscopic instruments, stapling devices, and suturing equipment. On the other hand, implantable devices are inserted into the body to assist in weight loss and promote satiety, such as gastric bands or balloons. These different types of equipment play crucial roles in the success of bariatric surgery procedures, offering patients less invasive options for weight management.</p></p>

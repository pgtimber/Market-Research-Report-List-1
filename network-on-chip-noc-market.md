<p><strong>The Network-on-Chip (NoC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Topology</li><li>Indirect Topology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Network-on-Chip (NoC) market types can be classified into two categories: Direct Topology and Indirect Topology. Direct Topology involves a network structure where each node is connected to a limited number of neighboring nodes, creating a more straightforward communication path. On the other hand, Indirect Topology allows for more complex routing by enabling nodes to communicate through multiple intermediate nodes. Both types offer different advantages and trade-offs in terms of scalability, latency, and power efficiency in NoC design.</p></p>

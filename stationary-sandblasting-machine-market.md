<p><p>Stationary sandblasting machines are available in two main types: dry and wet. In the dry market, abrasive material is propelled at high speeds using compressed air, creating a powerful blasting action for removing paint, rust, and other coatings from surfaces. On the other hand, the wet market involves the addition of water to the abrasive material, reducing dust and providing a more controlled and gentler blasting process. Both types offer unique benefits and are preferred for different applications based on the desired results and environmental considerations.</p></p>

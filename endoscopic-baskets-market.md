<p><p>Endoscopic baskets market offers various types to suit different requirements. Straight baskets are commonly used for retrieving large stones or foreign objects. Tipless baskets are designed to prevent mucosal damage during retrieval. Mesh baskets are ideal for grasping small stones or fragments. Helical baskets are effective for grasping slippery stones. Other types may include expandable or retrievable baskets. Each type serves a specific purpose in endoscopic procedures, providing physicians with a range of options for safe and efficient removal of obstructions or foreign bodies.</p></p>

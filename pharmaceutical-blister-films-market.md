<p><ul><li>PCTFE</li><li>PVDC</li><li>COC Cold Form Foil</li><li>PVC</li><li>PP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical blister films market types include PCTFE, PVDC, COC cold form foil, PVC, and PP. PCTFE offers high moisture barrier properties, while PVDC provides excellent oxygen barrier. COC cold form foil is known for its flexibility and compatibility with various medications. PVC films are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness, and PP films are preferred for their clarity and resistance to moisture. Each type of film is chosen based on the specific requirements for packaging pharmaceutical products effectively and safely.</p></p>

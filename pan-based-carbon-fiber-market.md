<p><ul><li>Small Tow</li><li>Big Tow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PAN-based carbon fiber market can be categorized into two types: small tow and big tow. Small tow carbon fibers are characterized by their smaller individual fiber diameter, typically ranging from 1K to 12K filaments. These fibers are widely used in applications such as sporting goods, aerospace, and automotive, as they offer enhanced mechanical properties. On the other hand, big tow carbon fibers have a larger individual fiber diameter, usually ranging from 24K to 50K filaments. These fibers are primarily used in industrial applications, including wind energy, pressure vessels, and infrastructure, due to their cost-effectiveness and high production capabilities.</p></p>

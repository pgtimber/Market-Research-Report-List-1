<p><p>A trunking system, also known as a private branch exchange (PBX) system, is a telecommunication system that allows multiple users within an organization to share a smaller number of communication channels or lines. It provides a more efficient way of communication by enabling the simultaneous transmission of voice and data traffic. Trunking systems are commonly used in organizations or companies with a large number of employees who require constant communication.</p><p>The trunking system market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. Factors such as the increasing demand for seamless communication and the rising adoption of cloud-based communication systems are driving market growth.

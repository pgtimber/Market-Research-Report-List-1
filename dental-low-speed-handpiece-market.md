<p><p>Dental low-speed handpieces are an essential tool in dental treatments. There are two main types: straight handpieces and bend handpieces. Straight handpieces have a straight design, allowing for easy access to posterior teeth. They are commonly used for polishing and applying dental materials. On the other hand, bend handpieces have a slight angle in their design, which enables better access to hard-to-reach areas. They are often used for endodontic procedures and surgical extractions. Overall, these two types of handpieces cater to different dental needs, providing dentists with versatility in their practice.</p></p>

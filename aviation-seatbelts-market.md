<p><p>Aviation seatbelts market comprises different types based on the number of attachment points. Two-point seatbelts have two attachment points, providing basic restraint. Three-point seatbelts feature an additional attachment point across the lap for increased safety. Four-point seatbelts include two shoulder and two lap attachment points, offering enhanced support. Other types of seatbelts may include five-point harnesses or inflatable restraints. These variations cater to different aircraft designs, passenger needs, and safety requirements in the aviation industry.</p></p>

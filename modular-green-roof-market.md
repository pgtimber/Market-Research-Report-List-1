<p><strong>The Modular Green Roof Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intensive</li><li>Semi-intensive</li><li>Extensive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intensive modular green roofs are characterized by a deeper soil depth, allowing for a wider variety of plants and vegetation that require more maintenance and irrigation. Semi-intensive green roofs fall in between intensive and extensive, with a medium soil depth and plant selection that requires moderate maintenance. Extensive green roofs have shallow soil depths and primarily consist of low-maintenance, drought-resistant plants. Each market type offers different benefits and considerations in terms of aesthetics, maintenance requirements, and environmental impact.</p></p>

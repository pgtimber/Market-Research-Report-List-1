<p><p>Light cure adhesives are classified into different types based on their composition and curing mechanism. Acrylate light cure adhesives are commonly used in bonding plastics and metals due to their high bond strength. Silicon-gel light cure adhesives are flexible and suitable for bonding materials with differing coefficients of thermal expansion. Anaerobic light cure adhesives are used in applications where air is limited, such as in tight-fitting assemblies. Other types of light cure adhesives include epoxy-based adhesives and UV-curable adhesives, each with unique properties and applications.</p></p>

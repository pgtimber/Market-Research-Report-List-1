<p><p>Quick release couplings with safety lock are used to quickly connect and disconnect fluid lines in various industries. There are two main market types based on the thread design: external thread and internal thread. External thread couplings have a threaded connection on the outside of the coupler, while internal thread couplings have a threaded connection on the inside. External thread couplings are commonly used in applications where the coupling needs to be connected to a male thread, while internal thread couplings are used when the coupling needs to be connected to a female thread.</p></p>

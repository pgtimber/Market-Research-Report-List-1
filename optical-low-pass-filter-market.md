<p><p>An Optical Low-pass Filter (OLPF) is a device used in digital cameras to reduce aliasing and moiré effects caused by high-resolution sensors. OLPFs are commonly found in DSLR cameras and high-end mirrorless cameras.</p><p>The Optical Low-pass Filter Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing adoption of digital cameras across various industries such as photography, healthcare, and automotive.

<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Grade Silicon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method</li><li>Float Zone (FZ) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Grade Silicon market can be categorized into two main types: Czochralski (CZ) Method and Float Zone (FZ) Method. </p><p>In the CZ method, silicon crystals are grown from a seed crystal submerged in molten silicon. This method is commonly used for large-scale production due to its efficiency in creating high-quality silicon wafers for optical applications. </p><p>On the other hand, the FZ method involves passing a floating zone of molten silicon along a rod to produce single crystal silicon with very low levels of impurities, making it ideal for high-performance optical devices.</p></p>

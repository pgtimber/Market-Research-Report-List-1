<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Patterned Wafer Inspection Equipment (OPWIE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bright-Field Inspection Systems</li><li>Dark-Field Inspection Systems</li><li>Electron Beam Inspection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Patterned Wafer Inspection Equipment (OPWIE) market includes Bright-Field Inspection Systems, Dark-Field Inspection Systems, and Electron Beam Inspection Systems. Bright-Field Inspection Systems use direct illumination to detect defects on wafers. Dark-Field Inspection Systems use angled illumination to highlight defects, while Electron Beam Inspection Systems use a focused electron beam to scan wafers for defects at a nanometer-scale resolution. These different types of inspection systems cater to varying needs in the semiconductor industry for thorough and precise wafer inspection.</p></p>

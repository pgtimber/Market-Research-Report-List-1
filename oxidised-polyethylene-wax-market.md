<p><p>Oxidized Polyethylene Wax is divided into two main types based on their density: High Density Wax and Low Density Wax. High Density Wax is denser and has a higher melting point, making it suitable for applications requiring a tougher wax with better heat resistance. Conversely, Low Density Wax is lighter and has a lower melting point, making it more flexible and easy to melt, suitable for uses where a softer wax is required. Both types offer different characteristics and are used in various industries for their specific properties.</p></p>

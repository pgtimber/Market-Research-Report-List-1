<p><ul><li>S-Band</li><li>L-Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Primary Surveillance Radar (PSR) systems can be classified based on the frequency band they operate in. S-Band radar operates in the 2-4 GHz frequency range and is known for its long-range coverage and high resolution. It is commonly used for air traffic control purposes. On the other hand, L-Band radar operates in the 1-2 GHz frequency range and is suitable for shorter-range surveillance, making it ideal for airport surface movement monitoring and weather tracking. Both S-Band and L-Band radars play a critical role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of air traffic operations.</p></p>

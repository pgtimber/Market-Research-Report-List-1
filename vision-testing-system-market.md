<p><strong>The Vision Testing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ffERG (Full Field Electroretinography)</li><li>mfERG (Multifocal Electroretinography)</li><li>ERG (Pattern Electroretinography)</li><li>VEP (Visual Evoked Potential)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vision testing system market types include ffERG, mfERG, ERG, VEP, and other specialized tests. ffERG measures the overall function of the retina, while mfERG provides detailed mapping of retinal function. ERG evaluates the response of the retina to patterned light stimuli. VEP assesses the functional integrity of the visual pathway from the retina to the visual cortex. Other tests may include contrast sensitivity testing, color vision testing, and dark adaptation tests to evaluate different aspects of visual function. These tests are essential in diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>Bouldering crash pads come in three main types based on their thickness: below 3 inches, 3.1 inch-4 inches, and above 4 inches. Below 3 inches pads are lightweight, portable, and suitable for low-level bouldering problems. The 3.1 inch-4 inch pads provide moderate cushioning and are ideal for medium-height falls. Pads above 4 inches offer maximum protection for highball bouldering and more challenging routes. Each type caters to different bouldering requirements, providing varying levels of impact absorption and safety.</p></p>

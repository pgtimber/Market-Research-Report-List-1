<p><p>Biofertilizers can be categorized into different types, including nitrogen-fixing, phosphate-solubilizing, potash-mobilizing, and others. Nitrogen-fixing biofertilizers contain nitrogen-fixing bacteria that help convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form plants can use. Phosphate-solubilizing biofertilizers contain microorganisms that release phosphorus, making it more available to plants. Potash-mobilizing biofertilizers contain bacteria that help plants absorb potassium more effectively. Other types of biofertilizers may include those that enhance plant growth, increase nutrient uptake, or improve soil health.</p></p>

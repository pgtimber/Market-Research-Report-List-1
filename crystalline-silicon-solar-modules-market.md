<p><p>Crystalline silicon solar modules are divided into two main types: polycrystalline and monocrystalline solar modules. Polycrystalline solar modules are made from multiple silicon crystals, resulting in a lower efficiency but a more cost-effective option. On the other hand, monocrystalline solar modules are made from a single silicon crystal, making them more efficient but also more expensive. Both types play a crucial role in the solar energy market, catering to different needs and budgets.</p></p>

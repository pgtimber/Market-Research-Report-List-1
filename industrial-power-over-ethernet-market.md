<p><strong>The Industrial Power Over Ethernet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment</li><li>Powered Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Power Over Ethernet (PoE) market consists of two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) and Powered Devices (PD). PSE refers to devices that provide power to PDs over Ethernet cables, such as PoE switches and midspans. PDs, on the other hand, are devices that receive power from the PSE, such as IP cameras, wireless access points, or VoIP phones. PSEs act as power suppliers, while PDs act as power receivers in the PoE ecosystem, enabling the transmission of both data and power over a single Ethernet connection.</p></p>

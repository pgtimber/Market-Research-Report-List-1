<p><p>The Automotive Engine Oil Coolant market consists of two main types of cooling systems: Air Cooled and Liquid Cooled. Air cooled systems utilize air flow to dissipate heat from the engine oil, while Liquid cooled systems use a liquid coolant, usually water or a mixture of water and antifreeze, to absorb and transfer heat away from the engine oil. Both systems have their own unique advantages and disadvantages, with air cooled systems being more simple and lightweight, while liquid cooled systems are more efficient at cooling the engine oil.</p></p>

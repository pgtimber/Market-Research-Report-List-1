<p><ul><li>No Wire Tube</li><li>Wire Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary drilling hoses come in two main types: no wire tube and wire tube. No wire tube hoses are constructed without any reinforcement wires, making them more flexible and lightweight. They are ideal for applications where flexibility is critical. On the other hand, wire tube hoses have steel wire reinforcement, providing added strength and resistance to high pressure and abrasive materials. They are commonly used in demanding drilling operations where durability and toughness are essential. Both types cater to different needs in the rotary drilling industry.</p></p>

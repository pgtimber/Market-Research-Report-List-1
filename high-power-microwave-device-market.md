<p><p>Active microwave devices in the high power microwave device market are those that require an external power source to function, such as magnetrons and klystrons. These devices are able to generate and amplify microwave frequencies for various applications. On the other hand, passive microwave devices do not require an external power source and instead rely on the incoming signal for operation, such as antennas and waveguides. Both types of devices play a crucial role in the high power microwave device market for different purposes.</p></p>

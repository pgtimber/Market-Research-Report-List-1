<p><strong>The Commercial Truck Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diagonal Tires</li><li>Radial Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial truck tires market consists of two main types: diagonal tires and radial tires. Diagonal tires are constructed with layers of fabric that run at a 45-degree angle to the tread. They are known for their durability and resistance to punctures. On the other hand, radial tires have steel belts that run perpendicular to the tread, providing better stability and fuel efficiency. Radial tires are popular in the market due to their longer lifespan and improved handling capabilities, making them a preferred choice for many truck owners.</p></p>

<p><p>Polyaspartic acid coatings are commonly used in two main market types: Pure polyurea and hybrid polyurea. Pure polyurea coatings are 100% polyaspartic acid and provide superior durability, chemical resistance, and fast curing times. On the other hand, hybrid polyurea coatings are a combination of polyaspartic acid and other resins, additives, or pigments. This gives hybrid polyurea coatings additional properties such as flexibility, UV stability, and enhanced aesthetics. Both pure polyurea and hybrid polyurea coatings are widely used in industrial, commercial, and residential applications for protecting various surfaces.</p></p>

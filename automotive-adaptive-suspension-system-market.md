<p><strong>The Automotive Adaptive Suspension System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-active Suspension System</li><li>Active Suspension System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Adaptive Suspension System market is divided into two main types: Semi-active Suspension System and Active Suspension System. The Semi-active Suspension System utilizes shock absorbers with adjustable damping, providing a varying level of stiffness based on road conditions and vehicle speed. The system uses sensors to monitor the road and adjust the damping levels accordingly, enhancing ride comfort and stability. On the other hand, the Active Suspension System includes electronic controls that continuously monitor and adjust the suspension's response to road conditions, ensuring optimal ride quality and handling.</p></p>

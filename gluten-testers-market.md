<p><p>The Gluten Testers Market consists of two main types: Gluten Sensors and Gluten Test Kits. Gluten Test Kits are typically larger kits that require a small sample of food to be tested for gluten using a chemical process. Both types of testers are designed to help those with gluten sensitivities or intolerances easily determine if their food contains gluten, enabling them to make informed choices about what they eat.</p></p>

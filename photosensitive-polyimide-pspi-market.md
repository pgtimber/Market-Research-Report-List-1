<p><strong>The Photosensitive Polyimide (PSPI) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photosensitive Polyimide</li><li>Negative Photosensitive Polyimide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photosensitive Polyimide (PSPI) is a type of material used in the electronics industry for its ability to be patterned using photolithography. Positive PSPI allows for the exposed areas to become soluble and be removed, while Negative PSPI allows for the unexposed areas to be removed instead. Positive PSPI is commonly used for creating circuit boards, while Negative PSPI is typically used for creating microfluidic devices. Both types of PSPI offer high resolution and excellent adhesion properties, making them ideal for various electronic applications.</p></p>

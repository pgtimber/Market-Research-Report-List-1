<p><p>Polyethersulfone (PES) dialyzers are medical devices used in hemodialysis to remove waste and excess fluid from the blood. The market for PES dialyzers can be categorized into two types: low flux membrane and high flux membrane. Low flux membrane dialyzers have smaller pore sizes, allowing for the removal of small waste molecules. High flux membrane dialyzers, on the other hand, have larger pore sizes, enabling the removal of both small and medium-sized waste molecules. These different types cater to the varying needs of patients depending on their specific filtration requirements.</p></p>

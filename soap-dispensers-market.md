<p><p>Soap dispensers market can be categorized into two main types: manual soap dispensers and automatic soap dispensers. Manual soap dispensers require users to physically press a button to dispense soap, while automatic soap dispensers use sensors to detect hands and dispense soap automatically. Manual soap dispensers are more common in homes and smaller businesses, while automatic soap dispensers are popular in public restrooms, hospitals, and other high-traffic areas where hygiene is crucial. Both types offer convenient and hygienic ways to access soap.</p></p>

<p><p>Agriculture packaging market includes various types of packaging solutions such as pouches, bags/bins, silo bags, clamshells, bottles, trays, bulk containers, and others. Pouches are flexible packages suitable for small agricultural products. Bags/bins are larger containers for storing bulk commodities. Silo bags are used for temporary storage of grains. Clamshells are commonly used for packaging fruits and vegetables. Bottles are used for packaging liquids like fertilizers. Trays are utilized for packaging seedlings or delicate produce. Bulk containers are designed for shipping and storing large quantities of agricultural products. Other types of packaging may include cartons, crates, or bins.</p></p>

<p><p>Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators (NiPPV) are devices used to deliver pressurized air into the lungs without the need for invasive procedures. There are two main types of NiPPV machines in the market - CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) and BiPAP (Bi-level Positive Airway Pressure). CPAP machines provide a continuous level of pressure to keep the airways open, while BiPAP machines offer varying levels of pressure during inhalation and exhalation. Both types are commonly used to treat sleep apnea and respiratory conditions.</p></p>

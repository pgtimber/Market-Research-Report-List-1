<p><ul><li>Flat Flange</li><li>Non-planar Flange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic flange gasket sheet market can be categorized into two main types based on the shape of the flange they are designed for: flat flange and non-planar flange. Flat flange gaskets are used in applications where the flange surfaces are parallel and do not have any irregularities. On the other hand, non-planar flange gaskets are designed for flanges with irregular or non-parallel surfaces. Both types of gaskets are crucial in providing a reliable seal between mating flange surfaces in various industrial applications.</p></p>

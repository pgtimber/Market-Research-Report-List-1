<p><strong>The Electrode Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cathode Electrode Sheets</li><li>Anode Electrode Sheets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrode Sheets Market consists of two main types: Cathode Electrode Sheets and Anode Electrode Sheets. Cathode Electrode Sheets are used in batteries and other energy storage devices to store and release electrons during the charging and discharging process. They are typically made from materials like lithium cobalt oxide or lithium iron phosphate. On the other hand, Anode Electrode Sheets are responsible for absorbing and storing electrons during charging. They are commonly made from materials such as graphite or silicon. Both types of electrode sheets are essential components in the manufacturing of batteries.</p></p>

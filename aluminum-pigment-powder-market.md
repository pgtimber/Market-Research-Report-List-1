<p><ul><li>Leafing Aluminum Pigment Powder</li><li>Non-leafing Aluminum Pigment Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum pigment powder is available in two main types: leafing and non-leafing. Leafing aluminum pigment powder consists of aluminum flakes that float or "leaf" on the surface of the coating, providing a bright and shiny finish. On the other hand, non-leafing aluminum pigment powder contains particles that sink into the coating, resulting in a more matte appearance. Both types have their own unique applications and characteristics, catering to different needs in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Passive Vehicle Access Systems refer to keyless entry systems that allow users to unlock and start their vehicles without actively interacting with the key. These systems use proximity sensors and wireless communication to detect the presence of the key fob and automatically unlock the doors or start the engine. Active Vehicle Access Systems, on the other hand, require users to actively engage with the key, such as pressing buttons to unlock or start the vehicle. Both types of systems enhance convenience and security for vehicle owners.</p></p>

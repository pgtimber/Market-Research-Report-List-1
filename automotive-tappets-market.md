<p><p>Automotive tappets are an essential component in engines that control the opening and closing of valves. Two main types of tappets are flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets have a flat contact surface with the camshaft lobe, while roller tappets have a roller at the end that reduces friction and wear. The flat tappet market is still popular in older engines, while the roller tappet market is gaining momentum in modern engines due to their improved performance and longevity.</p></p>

<p><p>Electric immersion heaters are used in various industries for heating liquids. Immersion heaters can be classified into different types based on their design and installation method. These types include immersion heaters, screw plug immersion heaters, flanged immersion heaters, over-the side immersion heaters, and others. Immersion heaters are submerged directly into the liquid to efficiently transfer heat, while screw plug immersion heaters are threaded into a tank or vessel. Flanged immersion heaters are installed by bolting onto a tank, while over-the side immersion heaters hang over the side of a tank.</p></p>

<p><p>Authentication software is a crucial tool used to verify the identity of users accessing computer systems or online platforms. There are three main types of authentication software in the market. Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security by requiring users to provide two different forms of identification, such as a password and a unique code sent to their mobile device. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) goes a step further, demanding additional authentication factors like biometrics or security questions. Lastly, single-factor authentication (SFA) only requires one form of identification, typically a password or PIN.</p></p>

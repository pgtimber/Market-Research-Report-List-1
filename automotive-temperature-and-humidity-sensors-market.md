<p><p>Automotive temperature and humidity sensors have two main types in the market: conventional sensors and digital sensors. On the other hand, digital sensors use digital technology to provide accurate and real-time data on temperature and humidity. They are more advanced, reliable, and easy to integrate into modern automotive systems.

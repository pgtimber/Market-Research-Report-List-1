<p><p>Refractory alumina market is segmented into different types such as calcined alumina, fused alumina, tabular alumina, and others. Calcined alumina is produced by heating alumina hydrate to high temperatures, while fused alumina is produced by fusing alumina in electric arc furnaces. Tabular alumina is a high-purity form of alumina produced by sintering alumina powder in a special furnace. Other types of refractory alumina include white fused alumina, brown fused alumina, and bubble alumina, each with its own unique properties and applications in the refractory industry.</p></p>

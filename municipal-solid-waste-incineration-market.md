<p><ul><li>Moving Grate</li><li>Rotary-kiln</li><li>Fluidized Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Municipal Solid Waste Incineration is a process that involves burning solid waste to generate energy. There are three main types of incineration technologies used in the market: Moving Grate, Rotary-kiln, and Fluidized Bed. Moving Grate incinerators use a continuously moving grate to burn waste. Rotary-kiln incinerators rotate waste in a drum to ensure complete combustion. Fluidized Bed incinerators suspend waste on a bed of air, allowing for efficient burning. Each type of incinerator has its own advantages and suitability for different kinds of waste materials.</p></p>

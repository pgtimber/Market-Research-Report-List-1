Private Cloud, on the other hand, is exclusive to a single organization, providing greater control and security. Hybrid Cloud combines elements of both, enabling organizations to integrate their private cloud with public cloud services, optimizing flexibility, and security based on individual requirements. These variations cater to different security needs and offer organizations a range of options for storing and managing their data securely in the cloud.</p></p>

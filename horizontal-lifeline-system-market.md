<p><p>Horizontal lifeline systems are essential safety equipment used in a variety of industries to protect workers at height. The four main types of horizontal lifeline systems are Gallows System, Lanyard System, Strap System, and Others. Gallows systems use a rigid structure for support, while lanyard systems involve attaching a lanyard to an anchor point. Strap systems use a flexible strap for support, and other systems include a combination of different components for safety. These systems provide reliable fall protection for workers at height.</p></p>

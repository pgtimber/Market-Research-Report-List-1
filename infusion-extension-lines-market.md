<p><ul><li>One-way Extension</li><li>Two-way Extension</li><li>Three-way Extension</li><li>Four-way Extension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infusion Extension Lines come in various types based on the number of channels they possess for delivering medication or fluids. One-way Extension lines allow for the continuous flow of fluids. Two-way Extension lines have an additional channel for medications to be administered. Three-way Extension lines include a third channel for additional medications or blood sampling. Four-way Extension lines offer even more versatility with four separate channels for fluid and medication delivery, and blood sampling. Each type serves a unique purpose in clinical settings for efficient patient care.</p></p>

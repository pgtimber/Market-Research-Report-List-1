<p><ul><li>Double Rollers</li><li>Four Rollers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal roller mills can be categorized into two primary types: double rollers and four rollers. Double roller mills contain two parallel rollers that rotate in opposite directions, crushing materials between them. On the other hand, four roller mills have four rollers positioned in a square arrangement to achieve finer and more efficient grinding. Both types are commonly used in industries such as mining, agriculture, and construction for grinding and crushing various materials to achieve the desired particle size for further processing.</p></p>

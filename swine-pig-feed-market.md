Pig grower feed is designed for pigs in the intermediate stage of growth to meet their nutritional requirements for muscle development and weight gain. Sow feed is tailored for pregnant and lactating sows to ensure optimal health and milk production. Each type of feed is formulated to meet the specific needs of pigs at different stages of growth.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Electrical Discharge Machining Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sinker EDM</li><li>Wire EDM</li><li>Hole Drilling EDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) services encompass three main types: sinker EDM, wire EDM, and hole drilling EDM. Sinker EDM, also known as die sinking or cavity type EDM, utilizes a specially shaped electrode to create desired shapes and features in a workpiece. Wire EDM employs a thin wire electrode that moves through the workpiece, allowing for precision cutting of complex shapes. Hole drilling EDM uses a cylindrical electrode to drill precise holes or small indentations in conductive materials. These EDM methods find applications across various industries for their ability to achieve high precision and intricate designs.</p></p>

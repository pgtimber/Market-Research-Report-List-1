<p><p>Cored solder wire is a type of solder wire that has a flux core inside it. This flux helps in the soldering process by removing oxides from the metals being joined, enhancing wetting, and minimizing the formation of bridges during soldering. Cored solder wire is widely used in the electronics industry for various applications such as surface mount technology, through-hole assembly, and rework and repair.</p><p>The cored solder wire market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.7% during the forecast period. <p><p>Cored Solder Wire is available in three main market types: No Clean, Water Soluble, and Rosin Based. </p><p>No Clean solder wire is designed to leave minimal to no residue after soldering, requiring little to no additional cleaning. </p><p>Water Soluble solder wire leaves a residue that can be easily cleaned with water, making it ideal for applications where cleanliness is crucial. </p><p>Rosin Based solder wire contains flux derived from rosin, providing good soldering performance and reliability. Each type offers unique benefits for different soldering applications.</p></p>

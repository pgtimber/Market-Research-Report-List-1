<p><p>The biodegradable plastic market can be categorized into different types based on the materials used for production. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is a bioplastic made from renewable resources like cornstarch or sugarcane. Polybutylene Succinate (PBS) is a compostable plastic derived from succinic acid and butanediol. Polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA) are biodegradable plastics produced by bacteria. Cellulose derivatives are made from cellulose fibers and are commonly used in packaging materials. Other types of biodegradable plastics include materials like starch blends and polycaprolactone.</p></p>

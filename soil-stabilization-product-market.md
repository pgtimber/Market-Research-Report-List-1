<p><p>Soil stabilization products are categorized into three main types: Polymers, Mineral & Stabilizing Agents, and Other Additives. Polymers are chemicals that improve the strength and durability of soil. Mineral & Stabilizing Agents include materials like lime, cement, and fly ash that help increase the load-bearing capacity of soil. Other Additives such as agricultural waste, sludge, chelates, and salts can also be used to enhance soil properties. These products play a crucial role in construction, agriculture, and environmental remediation projects.</p></p>

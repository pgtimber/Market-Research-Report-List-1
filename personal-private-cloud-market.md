<p><p>A personal cloud is a type of cloud storage service that is primarily focused on the needs of individual users. Unlike a public cloud, a private cloud is typically hosted on-premises or in a dedicated data center and is fully controlled and managed by the organization. Private clouds offer more control over data security and customization options compared to public clouds, making them ideal for businesses that have strict regulatory or compliance requirements.</p><p>The personal and private cloud market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years.

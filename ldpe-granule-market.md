<p><strong>The LDPE Granule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoclave Process</li><li>Tubular Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LDPE granule market can be categorized into two types based on the manufacturing processes - Autoclave Process and Tubular Process. Autoclave Process involves polymerization of ethylene under high pressure and temperature in a reactor, resulting in LDPE with high melt strength and good impact resistance. Tubular Process, on the other hand, involves continuous polymerization in a tubular reactor, producing LDPE with higher tensile strength and stiffness. Both processes have their unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>

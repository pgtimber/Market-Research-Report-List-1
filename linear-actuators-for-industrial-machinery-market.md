<p><p>Linear actuators are essential components in industrial machinery, allowing for precise and controlled linear motion. The market offers three primary types of linear actuators: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic actuators utilize compressed air to generate linear motion, making them suitable for applications requiring high speed and force. Electric actuators rely on electricity to convert rotational motion into linear motion, providing accurate and efficient performance. Hydraulic actuators use pressurized fluid to create linear movement, ideal for heavy-duty applications demanding substantial force. Each type has unique advantages and applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

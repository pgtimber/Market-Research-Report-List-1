<p><p>Uniform particle size ion exchange resins are categorized into four types based on their chemical properties: strong acid, weak acid, strong base, and weak base. Strong acid resins are capable of exchanging acidic ions, weak acid resins exchange weakly acidic ions, strong base resins exchange basic ions, and weak base resins exchange weakly basic ions. Each type has its unique applications in industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage production, due to their ability to efficiently remove specific ions from solutions.</p></p>

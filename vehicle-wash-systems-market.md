<p><ul><li>Dry Wash (Air Blower, Vacuum Based)</li><li>Wet Wash (Water, Liquid Soap &Shampoo Based)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle wash systems can be categorized into two main types: dry wash and wet wash. Dry wash systems use air blowers or vacuum-based methods to clean vehicles, eliminating the need for water and liquid soap. These systems are considered environmentally friendly as they conserve water and minimize chemical usage. On the other hand, wet wash systems utilize water, liquid soap, and shampoo-based solutions to clean vehicles. They are more traditional and widely used, but they require a significant amount of water and chemicals to effectively clean the vehicles.</p></p>

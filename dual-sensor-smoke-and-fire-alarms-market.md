<p><p>Dual-Sensor Smoke and Fire Alarms come in two main types: AC Type and DC Type. AC Type alarms are wired directly into the electrical system of a building, providing continuous power and often including a battery backup in case of power failure. DC Type alarms, on the other hand, operate solely on battery power and are typically used in situations where wiring into the electrical system is not feasible or preferred. Both types offer reliable detection of smoke and fire, providing crucial safety measures for residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>

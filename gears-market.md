<p><p>The gears market can be categorized into various types including Spur Gear, Helical Gear, Bevel Gear, Worm Gear, Gear Rack, and Others. Spur gears have straight teeth and are the most common type of gear used in applications requiring high efficiency. Helical gears have angled teeth for smoother and quieter operation. Bevel gears are used in applications where the direction of motion needs to be changed. Worm gears are used for high reduction ratios. Gear racks are used to convert rotary motion into linear motion. Other types of gears include planetary gears, spiral bevel gears, and internal gears.</p></p>

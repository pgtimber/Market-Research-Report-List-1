<p><p>Steel wire rope slings are available in two main market types: ungalvanized and galvanized. Ungalvanized steel wire rope slings are untreated and best suited for indoor use where moisture or corrosion is not a concern, while galvanized slings are coated with a protective layer to prevent rust and corrosion, making them ideal for outdoor applications or where exposure to moisture is likely.

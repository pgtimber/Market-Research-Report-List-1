<p><p>Automotive Grade Power Semiconductor Devices are categorized into different types based on their functions and features. Diode/Rectifier devices are used to regulate and convert electrical current. Thyristors are semiconductor devices that act as switches. MOSFETs are for controlling power in electrical circuits. IGBTs combine high input impedance of MOSFETs with low conduction loss of bipolar transistors. BJTs are used for amplification and switching applications. Each type plays a crucial role in the automotive industry, contributing to the efficiency and performance of vehicles.</p></p>

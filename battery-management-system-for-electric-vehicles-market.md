<p><strong>The Battery Management System for Electric Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized BMS</li><li>Distributed BMS</li><li>Modular BMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Management System (BMS) for Electric Vehicles (EVs) includes three types of markets - Centralized BMS, Distributed BMS, and Modular BMS. Centralized BMS refers to a system where the battery cells are connected to a central control unit. Distributed BMS involves multiple control units, with each unit managing a specific group of battery cells. Modular BMS consists of multiple independent modules, each managing a set of battery cells. These different market types offer various approaches for managing and monitoring the battery systems in electric vehicles.</p></p>

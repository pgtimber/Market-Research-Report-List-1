<p><p>Dental Preheating Ovens are used in dental laboratories to preheat dental restorations before further processing. The market for such ovens can be categorized into different types. Muffle ovens use a separate chamber to shield the restoration from direct flame or heat, ensuring uniform temperature distribution. Vacuum ovens create a vacuum inside the chamber to remove air and minimize oxidation during preheating. Infrared ovens employ infrared radiation for efficient heating. Microwave ovens use microwaves to generate heat. Other types may include hybrid ovens or those with additional features that cater to specific dental restoration requirements.</p></p>

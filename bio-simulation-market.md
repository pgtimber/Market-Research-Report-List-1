<p><p>Bio simulation, also known as biological simulation, is a technique that models biological processes using computer simulations. One such trend is the increasing adoption of in-silico trials, also known as virtual trials or digital twins. In-silico trials use computer models and simulations to predict the safety and efficacy of new drugs and treatments, reducing the need for extensive animal or human trials.</p><p>Another trend is the growing application of bio simulation in precision medicine.

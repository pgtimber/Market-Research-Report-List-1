<p><ul><li>Micro-Porous Hygiene Breathable Films</li><li>Non-Porous Hygiene Breathable Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hygiene Breathable Films are typically categorized into two types: Micro-Porous and Non-Porous films. Micro-Porous films are designed with small pores that allow for the passage of moisture vapor while blocking liquid and bacteria, making them suitable for applications like disposable diapers and sanitary napkins. On the other hand, Non-Porous films do not have pores and provide a complete barrier against both liquid and vapor, making them ideal for medical and industrial uses where protection is paramount.</p></p>

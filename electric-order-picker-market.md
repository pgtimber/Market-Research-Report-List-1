<p><p>An electric order picker, also known as a stock picker, is a piece of equipment commonly used in warehouses and distribution centers to efficiently pick and transport items from storage racks to fulfillment areas. As e-commerce continues to flourish, warehouses are under pressure to improve their picking and fulfillment operations, leading to a growing adoption of electric order pickers.</p><p>Moreover, the advantages offered by electric order pickers, such as increased efficiency, improved ergonomics, and reduced labor costs, are further driving the market growth.

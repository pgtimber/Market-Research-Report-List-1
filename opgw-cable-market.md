<p><ul><li>Layer Stranding Structure</li><li>Central Tube Design</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Layer Stranding Structure in OPGW cables involves layers of steel wires being stranded over the optical fibers to provide mechanical strength and protect the fibers. Central Tube Design, on the other hand, features a central tube where the optical fibers are housed, surrounded by layers of steel wires for protection. Both of these market types offer different solutions for the design and construction of OPGW cables, catering to diverse industry requirements and preferences.</p></p>

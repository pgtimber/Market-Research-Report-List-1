<p><p>Self expandable Metal Stents (SEMS) are medical devices used to treat strictures or blockages in various body ducts, such as the gastrointestinal tract, biliary system, and airways. These stents are made of metal mesh that can be expanded and implanted into the affected area to maintain patency and allow for normal flow of fluids or air.</p><p>The global Self expandable Metal Stents Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing prevalence of gastrointestinal disorders, growing aging population, technological advancements in stent design, and rising adoption of minimally invasive procedures.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Self expandable Metal Stents Market is the development of fully covered stents, which reduce the risk of tissue ingrowth and migration. Additionally, the market is witnessing a shift towards the use of biodegradable stents, which eliminate the need for stent removal procedures.</p><p>Overall, the Self expandable Metal Stents Market is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years, driven by the increasing demand for minimally invasive treatment options and the expanding applications of stent technology in healthcare.</p></p>

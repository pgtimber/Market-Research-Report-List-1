<p><p>Polyurethane topcoat market consists of two main types, water-based and oil-based topcoat. Water-based topcoats are environmentally friendly and have low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them a popular choice for indoor applications. Oil-based topcoats are known for their durability and resistance to water and chemicals, making them suitable for outdoor use. Both types provide a protective layer to seal and enhance the appearance of surfaces, but their differing compositions cater to different needs and preferences.</p></p>

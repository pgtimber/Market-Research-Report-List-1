<p><p>Flash storage market consists of various types, including NOR flash, NAND flash, Vertical NAND, and Floating-gate transistor. NOR flash is known for its fast read speeds and random access capabilities, making it suitable for code storage in devices. NAND flash offers high storage capacity at a lower cost, making it ideal for data storage in devices. Vertical NAND enables higher density and scalability compared to traditional NAND flash. Floating-gate transistor market utilizes floating-gate technology for high-speed programming and low power consumption in flash storage devices.</p></p>

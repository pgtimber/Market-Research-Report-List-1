<p><strong>The Automotive Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Fasteners</li><li>Non-threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive fasteners are essential components in vehicle assembly, securing different parts together. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts and screws, have helical ridges that create a secure connection by twisting into a pre-tapped hole or nut. Non-threaded fasteners, like clips and rivets, provide a fast and efficient way to join materials without the need for threading. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the structural integrity and safety of automobiles, with threaded fasteners offering adjustable tension and non-threaded fasteners providing quick and easy installation.</p></p>

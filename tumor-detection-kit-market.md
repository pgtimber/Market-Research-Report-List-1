<p><ul><li>ctDNA</li><li>CTC</li><li>Fluid Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tumor detection kits are available in three main types: ctDNA, CTC, and fluid detection. ctDNA kits detect tumor-specific genetic material that circulates in the blood, allowing for early diagnosis and tracking of treatment response. CTC kits isolate and identify rare tumor cells that are shed into the bloodstream, helping with cancer prognosis and treatment decisions. Fluid detection kits analyze bodily fluids, like urine or saliva, for tumor-specific biomarkers, providing a non-invasive method for early cancer detection. These kits play a vital role in improving cancer diagnosis, monitoring, and research.</p></p>

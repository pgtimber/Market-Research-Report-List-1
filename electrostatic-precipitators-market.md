<p><p>Electrostatic precipitators are devices used to remove particles and pollutants from industrial exhaust gases. They come in two main types: dry electrostatic precipitators and wet electrostatic precipitators. </p><p>Dry electrostatic precipitators use an electrostatic charge to collect particles on plates or tubes, while wet electrostatic precipitators use water or other liquid to scrub particles from the gas stream. Both types are effective in reducing emissions and improving air quality, with the choice between them depending on the specific application and needs of the industry.</p></p>

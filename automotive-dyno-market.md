<p><p>Automotive dyno market is segmented into two main types: engine dynamometer and chassis dynamometer. Engine dynamometer is used to measure and analyze the performance of a vehicle's engine, including power output, torque, and efficiency. This type of dyno is crucial for engine development and tuning. Chassis dynamometer, on the other hand, is used to test the overall performance of a vehicle, including acceleration, braking, and emissions.

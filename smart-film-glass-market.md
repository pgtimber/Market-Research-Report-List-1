<p><strong>The Smart Film Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart film glass market includes three main types: electrochromic smart glass, SPD smart glass, and PDLC smart glass. Electrochromic smart glass changes its tint based on the electrical charge applied to it, offering privacy and energy savings. SPD smart glass uses suspended particle technology to control the amount of light passing through, providing glare reduction and privacy. PDLC smart glass uses a film that changes from opaque to transparent when an electric current is applied, offering customizable privacy options.</p></p>

<p><p>Hygiene adhesives are classified into two main types - woven and non-woven. Woven hygiene adhesives are made from interlacing fibers in a crisscross pattern, providing strength and durability. Non-woven hygiene adhesives, on the other hand, are made from bonded fibers without weaving, making them more cost-effective and suitable for disposable products. Both types are commonly used in the manufacture of hygiene products such as diapers, feminine care products, and adult incontinence products.</p></p>

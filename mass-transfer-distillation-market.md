<p><p>Mass Transfer (Distillation) markets consist of Column Internals, Random Packing, Structured Packing, and Trays. Column Internals are components inside the distillation column that aid in separation. Random Packing is composed of randomly arranged packing material used to increase surface area for mass transfer. Structured Packing consists of specifically designed packing arrangements that improve efficiency in mass transfer processes. Trays are horizontal plates within the column that facilitate mass transfer through liquid-vapor contact. Each market type plays a unique role in optimizing distillation processes.</p></p>

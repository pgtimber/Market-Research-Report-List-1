<p><p>Automatic Opening Laparoscopic Retrieval Bags come in two main types: Detachable and Non-detachable. Detachable Retrieval Bags can be easily removed from the laparoscopic instrument after use, making them convenient for disposal and cleaning. Non-detachable Retrieval Bags are designed to stay attached to the instrument during the entire retrieval process, ensuring that the specimen remains secure until it is safely removed from the body. Both types offer their own set of advantages depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>

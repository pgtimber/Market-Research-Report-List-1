<p><p>HD maps market types can be divided into crowdsourcing model and centralized mode. In crowdsourcing model, data is collected from a large number of individual contributors, such as users' smartphones or connected vehicles, making it cost-effective and dynamic. On the other hand, centralized mode relies on dedicated data collection vehicles or aerial mapping services to gather data. It offers more accuracy and reliability but tends to be more expensive. Both market types have their own advantages and trade-offs, providing options for various applications and budgets in the HD maps industry.</p></p>

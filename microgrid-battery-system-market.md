<p><p>Microgrid Battery Systems are categorized into AC Microgrid Systems, DC Microgrid Systems, and Hybrid Microgrid Systems. AC Microgrid Systems use alternating current for distribution, making them compatible with existing power grids. DC Microgrid Systems use direct current for distribution, enabling easier integration with renewable energy sources. Hybrid Microgrid Systems combine both AC and DC elements to provide flexibility and efficiency in power distribution. Each type of system offers unique benefits and capabilities for different applications in the market.</p></p>

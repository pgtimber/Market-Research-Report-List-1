<p><p>Hotel room safes come in two main types: digital and mechanical. Digital safes are operated using a keypad or electronic locking system, providing quick and convenient access for guests. These safes often come with additional features such as audit trails and programmable codes. On the other hand, mechanical safes require a physical key or combination to open, offering a more traditional and reliable option.

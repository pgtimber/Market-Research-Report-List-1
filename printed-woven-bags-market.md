<p><p>Printed woven bags are a popular choice in the market and come in two types: laminated woven bags and non-laminated woven bags. Laminated woven bags are coated with a layer of plastic, making them more durable and resistant to moisture and tear. These bags are suitable for carrying heavy items and are commonly used in industries such as agriculture and construction. Non-laminated woven bags, on the other hand, do not have a plastic coating but are still strong and versatile. They are commonly used in retail and packaging applications.</p></p>

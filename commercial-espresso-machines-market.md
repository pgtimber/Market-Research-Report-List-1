<p><p>Commercial espresso machines can be categorized into three main types based on their operation: manually operated, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manually operated machines require the barista to control the brewing process, including timing and water pressure. Semi-automatic machines automate some aspects of the brewing process, such as pump pressure, but still require manual input from the barista. Fully automatic machines automate the entire brewing process, from grinding the beans to brewing the espresso, with minimal input needed from the barista.</p></p>

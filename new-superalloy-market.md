<p><p>The new superalloy market can be categorized into five main types: powder superalloy, intermetallic compound, ODS (Oxide Dispersion Strengthened) alloy, high temperature metal self-moistening materials, and other market offerings. Intermetallic compounds are formed by the bonding of multiple metallic elements, providing excellent mechanical properties. ODS alloys incorporate oxide particles for enhanced strength. High temperature metal self-moistening materials can maintain their mechanical properties at high temperatures.

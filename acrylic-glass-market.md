<p><strong>The Acrylic Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Cast Acrylic Glass</li><li>Continuous Cast Acrylic Glass</li><li>Extruded Cast Acrylic Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic glass is available in three main types: cell cast, continuous cast, and extruded cast. Cell cast acrylic glass is produced by pouring liquid acrylic into a mold and allowing it to harden. Continuous cast acrylic glass is made by continuously pouring liquid acrylic between two sheets of glass and then cooling it. Extruded cast acrylic glass is created by pushing the liquid acrylic through a die to form a continuous sheet. Each type of acrylic glass has its own unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>

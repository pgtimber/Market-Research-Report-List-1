<p><p>CO2 vaporizers come in three main types: Water Heated CO2 Vaporisers, Ambient Heated CO2 Vaporisers, and Electrically Heated CO2 Vaporisers. Water Heated CO2 Vaporisers use hot water to vaporise the CO2, providing a cost-effective and efficient solution. Ambient Heated CO2 Vaporisers rely on the surrounding environment to heat the CO2, making them suitable for areas with limited resources. Electrically Heated CO2 Vaporisers use electricity to heat the CO2, offering a consistent and reliable method of vaporization. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>

<p><p>Air-powered dock levelers are commonly found in two main types in the market: mobile dock levelers and fixed dock levelers. Mobile dock levelers are portable and can be easily moved from one loading bay to another, making them ideal for facilities with changing needs. Fixed dock levelers, on the other hand, are permanently installed at a specific loading bay and are designed for long-term use. Both types offer the benefit of providing a smooth transition between the loading dock and the delivery truck, improving efficiency and safety in the loading process.</p></p>

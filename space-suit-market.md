<p><ul><li>IVA (Intravehicular Activity) Suit</li><li>EVA (Extravehicular Activity) Suit</li><li>IEVA (Intra/Extravehicular Activity) Suit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The space suit market can be classified into three main types: IVA (Intravehicular Activity) suits are designed for use inside spacecraft, providing essential life support systems. EVA (Extravehicular Activity) suits are meant for astronauts to carry out tasks outside of the spacecraft, allowing them to survive and work in the harsh conditions of outer space. IEVA (Intra/Extravehicular Activity) suits combine features of both IVA and EVA suits, providing flexibility for astronauts to transition between tasks inside and outside the spacecraft.</p></p>

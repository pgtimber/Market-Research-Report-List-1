<p><p>Diving arm mixers are classified into two main types: double arms and single arm mixers. </p><p>Double arm mixers have two mixing arms that move in opposite directions, providing thorough and efficient mixing of ingredients. These mixers are ideal for heavy-duty production and large batches. </p><p>Single arm mixers have only one mixing arm that rotates in a circular motion, making them suitable for smaller batches and lighter mixing tasks. Both types of mixers are commonly used in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>

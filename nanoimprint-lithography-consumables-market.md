<p><p>Nanoimprint lithography consumables refer to materials essential for the process of nanoimprint lithography. There are three main types of consumables: Nano imprint resin, resist, and template. Nano imprint resin is used as the imprinting material, resist is used for patterning, and template is used to transfer the desired pattern onto the substrate. These consumables are crucial for achieving high-resolution patterns in nanoimprint lithography and are in high demand in various industries such as semiconductors, photonics, and nanotechnology.</p></p>

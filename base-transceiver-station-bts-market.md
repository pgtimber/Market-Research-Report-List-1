On-Grid BTS are directly connected to the main power supply and are commonly used in urban areas with stable electricity supply. Off-Grid BTS operate independently from the main power grid and rely on alternative energy sources like solar panels or batteries, making them suitable for remote or off-grid locations.</p></p>

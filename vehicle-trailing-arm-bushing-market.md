<p><ul><li>Trailing Arm Bushing</li><li>Semi Trailing Arm Bushing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trailing arm bushings are designed to connect the trailing arm to the vehicle frame, providing support and allowing for movement. Semi-trailing arm bushings serve a similar function, connecting the semi-trailing arm to the frame. The main difference lies in their design and the amount of movement they allow. Trailing arm bushings typically provide more flexibility and are commonly used in vehicles with independent rear suspension, while semi-trailing arm bushings are found in vehicles with semi-independent rear suspension systems. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining suspension alignment and handling.</p></p>

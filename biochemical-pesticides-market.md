<p><p>Biochemical pesticides are classified into two main types based on their physical form: liquid and powder. Liquid biochemical pesticides are formulated as solutions or emulsions that can be easily sprayed onto crops. They provide quick and uniform coverage, making them ideal for controlling pests on a large scale. On the other hand, powder biochemical pesticides come in the form of dry, granular particles that can be easily mixed with water to create a spray. They are convenient for storage and transportation and have a longer shelf life compared to liquid pesticides.</p></p>

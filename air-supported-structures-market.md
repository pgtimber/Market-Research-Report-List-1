<p><ul><li>Air-Supported Structures</li><li>Air-Inflated Structures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air-Supported Structures are large, clear-span buildings that are supported by pressurized air within a fabric envelope. These structures are commonly used for sports facilities, event venues, and warehouses. On the other hand, Air-Inflated Structures are lightweight, temporary structures that are inflated with air to create a rigid form. These structures are often used for outdoor events, advertising, and emergency shelters. Both types of structures offer quick installation, cost-effectiveness, and versatility in design.</p></p>

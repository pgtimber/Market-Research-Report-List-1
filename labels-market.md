<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure-sensitive Labels</li><li>Glue-applied Labels</li><li>Sleeve Labels</li><li>In-mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure-sensitive labels are self-adhesive labels that are applied to products by pressing them onto the surface. Glue-applied labels require a specific type of adhesive to adhere to the product. Sleeve labels are made from heat shrink film and are slipped over containers before being heat shrunk to fit securely. In-mold labels are applied to products during the manufacturing process, where the label becomes part of the final product. Each type of label offers unique benefits and is suited for different packaging applications.</p></p>

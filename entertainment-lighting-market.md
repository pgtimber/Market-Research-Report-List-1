<p><p>Entertainment lighting market can be categorized into three main types based on the source of light used. Thermal Radiation Source lighting relies on heat emission from a source like an incandescent bulb. Discharge Light Source lighting uses gas discharge or plasma to produce light, commonly seen in fluorescent or neon lighting. Electroluminescent Light market utilizes electricity to emit light through a material's luminescent properties. Each of these types offers unique features and benefits for various entertainment lighting applications.</p></p>

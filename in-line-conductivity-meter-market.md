<p><p>The in-line conductivity meter market is divided into two main types: single in-line conductivity meter and dual in-line conductivity meter. Single in-line conductivity meters are designed to measure the conductivity of a single solution at a time, while dual in-line conductivity meters are capable of measuring the conductivity of two separate solutions simultaneously. Both types of meters are commonly used in industrial processes, water treatment plants, and laboratories to monitor and control the conductivity levels of liquids for various applications.</p></p>

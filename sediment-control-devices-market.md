<p><p>Sediment control devices are used to prevent erosion and protect water quality on construction sites. The market types include silt fence, dewatering bags, filters, and others. Silt fences are temporary barriers made of geotextile fabric that trap sediment in runoff water. Dewatering bags are used to filter out sediment from water being discharged. Filters are devices that remove sediment particles from stormwater. Other types of sediment control devices include berms, check dams, and inlet protection devices.</p></p>

<p><p>Perfluorosulfonic acid ion-exchange resin is typically available in two main types: perfluorosulfonic acid resin dispersion and perfluorosulfonic acid resin granules. The dispersion form consists of small resin particles suspended in a liquid, making it ideal for applications requiring a uniform coating or layer. On the other hand, the granules form consists of larger resin particles that are suitable for bulk applications or when precise control over resin quantity is necessary. Both types offer efficient ion-exchange capabilities and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Self-Propelled Combine Harvesters</li><li>Tractor-Pulled Combine Harvesters</li><li>Power Take Off (PTO)-Powered Combine Harvesters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Combine harvester market is segmented into three main types. Self-propelled combine harvesters are equipped with their own engines for harvesting. Tractor-pulled combine harvesters are towed by a separate tractor for operation. Power Take Off (PTO)-Powered combine harvesters rely on the power generated by the tractor's engine through a PTO shaft. Each type offers different benefits and capabilities catering to various agricultural needs and farm sizes. Customers can choose the type that best suits their requirements for efficient harvesting.</p></p>

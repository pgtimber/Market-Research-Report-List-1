<p><p>Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR) Evaporators are used in two main market types: Compressed Steam System and Water Vapor Distillation System. The Compressed Steam System involves compressing steam generated during the evaporation process to reuse as a heat source, improving energy efficiency. On the other hand, the Water Vapor Distillation System utilizes the vapor pressure difference between water and a volatile solvent to separate them through distillation. Both market types offer efficient and environmentally friendly solutions for the evaporation process.</p></p>

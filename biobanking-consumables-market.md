<p><p>Biobanking consumables refer to the essential supplies and materials used in biobanks for the storage and preservation of biological samples. Some common biobanking consumables include vials, tubes, plates, sample collection and storage kits, racks, and labels.</p><p>The global biobanking consumables market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years. Several factors contribute to this growth, including the increasing emphasis on precision medicine, rising demand for personalized therapies, and advancements in biobanking technologies. The pandemic has highlighted the importance of biobanking for disease research, diagnostic development, and vaccine testing.

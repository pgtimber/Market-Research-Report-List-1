<p><p>Ultrasound Assisted Liposuction (UAL) machines are used in cosmetic surgery for body contouring procedures. Internal UAL machines utilize ultrasound energy delivered through a small probe inserted into the targeted area, helping to break down fat cells for easier removal. External UAL machines, on the other hand, emit ultrasound waves through a handheld device applied externally to the skin, assisting in the liquefaction of fat cells for suction. Both types of UAL machines aim to enhance the effectiveness and precision of liposuction procedures with the assistance of ultrasound technology.</p></p>

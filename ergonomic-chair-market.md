<p><ul><li>2-3 Degrees of Freedom Adjustment</li><li>Above 3 Degrees of Freedom Adjustment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ergonomic chairs with 2-3 degrees of freedom adjustment feature basic adjustments like seat height, backrest tilt, and armrest height. These chairs are suitable for individuals who require minimal customization for their seating comfort. On the other hand, ergonomic chairs with above 3 degrees of freedom adjustment provide advanced customization options such as seat depth, lumbar support, and tilt tension control. They are ideal for users who need extensive adjustability to address specific ergonomic requirements and ensure optimal comfort and support throughout the day.</p></p>

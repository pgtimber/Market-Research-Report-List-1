The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period. Manufacturers are constantly innovating to develop more advanced and user-friendly prostheses, incorporating features such as sensors, advanced control systems, and improved durability.</p><p>Moreover, the adoption of digital technologies such as 3D printing is also contributing to the growth of the arm prostheses market by enabling customized and more affordable prosthetic solutions. Overall, the arm prostheses market is expected to continue expanding as technology continues to evolve and enhance the lives of individuals with limb loss.</p></p>

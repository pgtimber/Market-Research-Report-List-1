<p><ul><li>Clicker Presses</li><li>Rotary Die Cutters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die cutting equipment is commonly classified into two main types: Clicker Presses and Rotary Die Cutters. Clicker presses are hydraulic machines that use a flat die to cut shapes out of materials such as leather, fabric, paper, and plastic. On the other hand, rotary die cutters use a cylindrical die to cut shapes at a high speed, making them suitable for mass production. Both types of equipment are widely used in industries such as packaging, automotive, and textiles for precise and efficient cutting processes.</p></p>

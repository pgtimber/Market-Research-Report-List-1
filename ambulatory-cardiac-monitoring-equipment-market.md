<p><p>The ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment market is composed of various types of devices used to monitor and record heart activity outside of a healthcare facility. Holter monitors are portable devices worn by patients that continuously record electrical activity of the heart over 24-48 hours. Event monitors are similar but are activated by the patient when symptoms occur. Mobile cardiac telemetry involves wireless transmission of real-time heart data for remote monitoring. Other types of ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment include loop recorders and external wearable patches, offering different features and monitoring durations.</p></p>

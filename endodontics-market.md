<p><p>Endodontics market consists of two main types: consumables and instruments. Consumables are the materials used in endodontic procedures, such as filling materials, irrigating solutions, and medicaments. These products are essential for the cleaning, shaping, and filling of root canals. On the other hand, instruments are the tools used by endodontists to perform these procedures, including rotary files, hand instruments, and apex locators. Both consumables and instruments play a crucial role in ensuring successful and efficient endodontic treatments.</p></p>

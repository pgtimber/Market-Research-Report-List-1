<p><p>The pharmaceutical equipment assembly market consists of two main types: full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic assembly refers to a process where the equipment is designed to operate entirely without human intervention. It is capable of executing tasks without any manual assistance, improving efficiency and reducing errors. On the other hand, semi-automatic assembly involves a combination of human intervention and automated processes. This type of assembly requires some degree of manual operation, supplementing the automated functions. Both types play a crucial role in the pharmaceutical industry, ensuring efficient and precise equipment assembly.</p></p>

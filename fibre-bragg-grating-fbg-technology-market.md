<p><p>Fibre Bragg Grating (FBG) Technology is a sensing technology that utilizes an optical fiber with a periodic variation in the refractive index to reflect specific wavelengths of light. This technology is commonly used in various industries such as telecommunications, oil and gas, aerospace, and healthcare for applications such as strain and temperature sensing, shape monitoring, and vibration analysis.</p><p>The global Fibre Bragg Grating (FBG) Technology Market is expected to experience substantial growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for advanced sensing technologies in various industries is one of the primary factors driving market growth. FBG technology offers several advantages over traditional sensing methods, such as high accuracy, reliability, and the ability to measure multiple parameters simultaneously.</p><p>Moreover, the adoption of FBG technology in the healthcare sector for applications like monitoring of vital signs, gait analysis, and rehabilitation is further propelling market growth. The increasing focus on structural health monitoring in critical infrastructure, such as bridges and pipelines, is also creating lucrative opportunities for the FBG technology market.</p><p>In addition, the market is witnessing the emergence of new trends.

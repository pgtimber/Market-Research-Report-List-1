<p><p>Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC) are advanced materials that consist of ceramic fibers embedded in a ceramic matrix. These composites have various types based on the material used for the matrix, such as oxide, Silicon Carbide (SiC), carbon, and others. Oxide-based CMCs are known for their high temperature resistance, while SiC-based CMCs offer excellent thermal conductivity. Carbon-based CMCs are lightweight and have high mechanical strength. Other types of CMCs incorporate different materials to provide unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>

<p><p>An extended stay hotel, also known as a serviced apartment or apart-hotel, is a type of accommodation that offers furnished rooms or apartments with amenities for long-term or temporary stays. Additionally, the increasing trend of relocating for job opportunities and the need for temporary accommodation during house renovations or relocations have also boosted the market.</p><p>Furthermore, the latest trends in the extended stay hotel market include the incorporation of technology-driven solutions to enhance the guest experience. Additionally, sustainability initiatives such as energy-efficient practices and eco-friendly amenities are gaining prominence in the market.</p><p>In conclusion, the extended stay hotel market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period.

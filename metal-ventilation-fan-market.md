<p><p>Metal ventilation fan market can be categorized into two main types: axial and centrifugal fans. Axial fans are designed to move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them suitable for applications requiring high airflow at lower pressures. On the other hand, centrifugal fans are designed to move air perpendicular to the fan's axis, allowing for higher pressure capabilities. Both types of fans are commonly used in industrial and commercial settings for ventilation and air circulation purposes, but their designs and functionalities cater to different needs and requirements.</p></p>

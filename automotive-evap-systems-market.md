<p><strong>The Automotive EVAP Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Tank</li><li>Gas Cap</li><li>Liquid Vapor Separator</li><li>Control Valve</li><li>Sensor</li><li>Hoses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive EVAP systems are crucial for controlling vehicle emissions. The market types for these systems include fuel tanks, gas caps, liquid vapor separators, control valves, sensors, and hoses. The fuel tank stores fuel and prevents vapors from escaping. The gas cap seals the fuel tank to prevent emission leakage. The liquid vapor separator removes liquid fuel from the vapor before it reaches the engine. Control valves regulate the flow of vapors. Sensors monitor the system, while hoses connect the components for a complete EVAP system.</p></p>

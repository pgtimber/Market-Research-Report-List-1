<p><p>Protective gloves market can be categorized into two main types: disposable gloves and reusable gloves. Disposable gloves are typically made of latex, nitrile, or vinyl materials and are designed for single-use applications to prevent contamination and reduce the risk of infection. Reusable gloves, on the other hand, are usually made of more durable materials like leather or rubber and can be washed and reused multiple times. Both types of gloves serve the common purpose of protecting hands from chemicals, germs, and other harmful substances.</p></p>
